Detailing

Full Interior Package

Thorough wipe down all interior surfaces. Vacuum, clean/deodorize carpets and upholstery. Blow out vents, clean/condition leather seats, steam clean all door jams/kick panels. *Additional fee applied for excessive dirt and pet hair.

Full Exterior Package

Decontamination wash, rims cleaned, clay bar treatment, wax applied by polisher. Dressing applied to tires and exterior trim. Hydrophobic sealant applied to exterior windows and paint to add a layer of gloss and protection.

Vinyl Wraps

Full Wraps

Full wraps will cover entire car. Browse our in-store selection of wraps or bring your own wraps. We work with most well known brands.

Partial Wraps

Partial wraps will cover a certain part of the car from customer's choice of location. Either browse our in-store selection of wraps or bring your own.

Paintjobs

Full Paint and Coatings

We do complete paintjobs and coatings to protect your paint. Liquid polymer coating is applied by hand to all exterior surfaces of vehicle, adding proecting from external paint damage and harmful UV rays.

Paint Correction

75-80% Defect Removal: Clay bar treatment, multi-stage polishing process to remove any oxidattion, defects(swirls). Paint glaze, and sealant applied to enhance and protect paint form future imperfection.
